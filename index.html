Grain is a creative R&D Laboratory.

We are an AI consultancy built for filmmakers, anchored by a partnership with SMUGGLER — one of North America's most awarded commercial production companies.

We believe the filmmakers most impacted by generative AI should be the ones shaping how it enters professional production. Our work is about fluency, not adoption. We meet filmmakers in their own creative processes and translate the technical landscape into terms that match how they already think and work.

What We Do

We educate filmmakers on the frontier capabilities of AI systems. By offering a direct connection between the latest research and the filmmaking community, we build conversation and collaboration organically before addressing specific client needs.

We're also cross-platform by design. We maintain active relationships across the model, research, and platform landscape so that the tools we recommend and pipelines we custom build are specifically tailored to the job.

Process Over Product

Most AI studios build a single product and expect filmmakers to adapt. Grain works the other way — building systems and pipelines that adapt to filmmakers.

Partners & Collaborators

Grain is the AI partner to SMUGGLER, building bespoke workflows for their directors across campaigns for the world's biggest brands. Our joint work extends into live event and immersive contexts, where we're developing real-time generative systems for stage and installation environments.

We work with OpenAI on bringing generative tools into the hands of professional filmmakers through workflow integration salons, summits, and hands-on workshops — with a focus on teaching filmmakers to build their own tools rather than adopt someone else's.

We collaborate with researchers in and around the Fluid Interfaces Group on generative modalities beyond image and video, including early exploration of EEG-to-video pipelines and the broader question of how consciousness, perception, and generative media intersect.

Our engagements are built around these commitments and unfold in two phases. Most begin in the first.

Phase 1

Fluency & Strategy

A collaborative engagement that orients a filmmaking team inside the current generative landscape. We lead creative ideation, visual research, and workflow exploration — reframing budgets where generative approaches unlock visions traditional production can't afford — and surface creative modalities that don't yet exist in the current playbook. Phase 1 is self-contained: teams leave with a clear map of what's possible and what's worth their attention.

Phase 2

Systems & Production

When a project is ready to move from exploration into execution, we build custom systems scoped to its exact needs — pipeline design, workflow integration, and on-set collaboration with existing craft departments. Phase 2 is structured per project, priced independently, and draws on additional production partners on an as-needed basis.

Every engagement begins with a conversation. Most start in Phase 1 and grow from there.
